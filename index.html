< About >

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Frank, a passionate software developer proudly based in Cleveland, Ohio. While I'm a native of Cleveland, my professional journey has taken me across the country, from New Jersey to California, where I've called home for the past six years. Despite my love for California, my beautiful wife and I decided to return to "The Land" to be closer to our families.

My career hasn't always been rooted in the tech world. I spent a significant part of my professional life working hands-on in the trades, contributing to remarkable projects and gaining invaluable insights into the construction and maintenance of our infrastructure.

Why the shift to a tech career? Well, my fascination with technology has been a longstanding passion. I dabbled in website creation and, as I delved deeper into coding in my spare time, my curiosity grew. When the opportunity arose to pursue tech full-time, I seized it without hesitation.

This website is a testament to my journey. Rather than a traditional resume, I've crafted it from scratch to showcase my skills, who I am, and what I love. Thanks for stopping by!
